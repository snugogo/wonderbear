## 12 页文本(中英双语)

### Page 1 — 开场:朵拉看见小云在哭
- **zh**:朵拉坐在窗边,看见一朵小云在天上哭。"咦,云为什么哭呢?"她小声问。
- **en**:Dora sat by the window. She saw a little cloud crying in the sky. "Why is the cloud crying?" she whispered.

### Page 2 — 引入冲突:云的难题
- **zh**:"我不想下雨,"小云抽抽噎噎地说,"下雨会弄湿小蚂蚁的鞋子,他们会感冒的。"
- **en**:"I don't want to rain," sobbed the little cloud. "If I rain, the ants' shoes will get wet, and they will catch a cold."

### Page 3 — WonderBear 第 1 次出场
- **zh**:朵拉皱起眉头,这个难题不简单。她最好的朋友 WonderBear 跑过来,圆滚滚的肚子一颤一颤。
- **en**:Dora frowned. This was a tricky problem. Her best friend WonderBear ran over, his round belly bouncing.

### Page 4 — WonderBear 提议
- **zh**:WonderBear 挠挠头说:"我们可以爬上去问问云吗?也许云需要一个朋友来帮忙。"
- **en**:WonderBear scratched his head. "Can we climb up and ask the cloud? Maybe the cloud needs a friend to help."

### Page 5 — 爬上月光
- **zh**:朵拉踩着月光,一步一步爬到天上。月亮像一座银白色的桥,通向云的家。
- **en**:Dora stepped on the moonlight, climbing higher and higher. The moon was like a silver bridge leading to the cloud's home.

### Page 6 — 真相揭开(转折点)
- **zh**:"其实我喜欢一朵叫小白的云,"小云脸红了,"可是下了雨,我们就要分开。我不想离开他。"
- **en**:"I actually love a cloud named Little White," blushed the cloud. "But if I rain, we have to say goodbye. I don't want to leave him."

### Page 7 — 朵拉沉思
- **zh**:朵拉坐在云朵上,看着远方的小白。她在想:有没有一个地方,既需要雨,又不会有蚂蚁?
- **en**:Dora sat on the cloud, looking at Little White in the distance. She thought hard: is there a place that needs rain, but has no ants?

### Page 8 — 灵机一动(高潮 1)
- **zh**:朵拉突然眼睛一亮:"沙漠!沙漠正好需要雨!那里没有蚂蚁,只有仙人掌在等水喝。"
- **en**:Dora's eyes lit up. "The desert! The desert needs rain! There are no ants there, only cactus plants waiting for water."

### Page 9 — 小云欢欣
- **zh**:小云开心地跳起来,眼泪变成了金色的小星星,洒满整个天空。
- **en**:The little cloud jumped with joy. Its tears turned into golden stars, sparkling across the whole sky.

### Page 10 — 一起飞向沙漠(高潮 2)
- **zh**:小云带着朵拉一起飞向沙漠。一路上,小白也跟了过来,两朵云手牵手在风里飞。
- **en**:The cloud took Dora flying toward the desert. Along the way, Little White followed, and the two clouds held hands in the wind.

### Page 11 — 沙漠下雨(结尾 1)
- **zh**:沙漠下起了温柔的雨。仙人掌张开嘴巴,咕嘟咕嘟喝个不停,开出了红色的花。
- **en**:Gentle rain fell on the desert. The cactus plants drank and drank, and bloomed beautiful red flowers.

### Page 12 — 圆满温暖(结尾 2)
- **zh**:小云和小白手拉手,在彩虹下飞舞。朵拉笑得真开心 — 帮朋友找到办法,是世界上最棒的事。
- **en**:The two clouds danced under the rainbow, hand in hand. Dora laughed with joy — helping a friend find a way is the best feeling in the world.
